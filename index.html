xlunlokr

Remove sheet & workbook protection from Excel files.

🔒 Your files never leave your browser — everything runs locally.

Drop Excel files here or click to choose — .xlsx, .xlsm, .xltx, .xltm

    Removes edit protection (sheetProtection / workbookProtection) only. It cannot decrypt files that need a password to open. Use it on files you're allowed to modify.
